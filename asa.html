Tell Me About The Opportunity:

The Egbin PowerUp Internship Programme is a paid industrial training programme designed for Nigerian undergraduates. Hosted by Egbin Power Plc, West Africa’s largest power generation station (1,320 MW capacity), the programme provides students with hands‑on experience, career development, and professional networking in the energy sector.

Which Fields are Eligible?

  • Engineering
  • Chemistry
  • Computer Science

Type:

  • Undergraduate Internship | Paid | Nigeria

Who can apply?

Applicants must:

  • Be undergraduate Nigerian students.
  • Possess a minimum of a second-class upper (2:1) or equivalent.

How are applicants selected?

  • Applications reviewed online.
  • Shortlisting based on academic performance and field of study.
  • Selected candidates invited to join the internship cohort at Egbin Power Plc.

Which Countries Are Eligible?

  • Nigeria only.

Where will the opportunity be taken?

  • Egbin Power Plc, Lagos State, Nigeria.

How Many Positions?

  • Multiple internship slots available across eligible fields.

What is the benefit of the opportunity?

  • Hands‑on practical training in power generation.
  • Monthly stipends during internship.
  • Career development opportunities.
  • Professional networking with industry experts.

How Long Will the Program Last?

  • Duration not specified, but typically aligned with industrial training periods (several months).

How to Apply:

  1. Review eligibility criteria.
  2. Visit the application webpage.
  3. Fill out the online application form accurately.
  4. Submit before June 16, 2026.
  5. Await contact from Egbin Power for next steps.
